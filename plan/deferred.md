# Deferred / Unscheduled Backlog

**Requirements reference:** [docs/requirements.md §3](../docs/requirements.md)

Items deliberately deferred during the requirements workshop — not rejected, just not yet assigned to a phase. Promote an item to its own `plan/phase-N-*.md` file when it's actually being scoped.

- Mobile/touch support
- Driving aids (ABS, traction control, etc.)
- Rebindable controls
- Multiplayer
- 2D HUD (lap timer, speed, minimap) beyond the in-cockpit dashboard
- Aerodynamics/downforce, tire wear, fuel load, mechanical damage in the physics model
